Southern Expressway – Sri Lanka

The Southern Expressway (E01) is Sri Lanka’s first expressway connecting Colombo with Galle, Matara and Hambantota. It was constructed to reduce travel time, improve transportation efficiency and support economic development in the Southern region. The Gelanigama to Pinnaduwa section includes major interchanges such as Dodangoda, Welipenna, Kurundugahahetekma and Baddegama.

Road Risk Map (Gelanigama to Pinnaduwa)

Yellow – Low Risk | Orange – Moderate Risk | Red – High Risk

Methodology

• Expressway centerline extracted from OpenStreetMap

• Points generated at 100m intervals

• Chainage calculated for each point

• IRI values assigned using chainage

• Road curvature calculated using angle

Risk = 0.7 (IRI/5) + 0.3 (Angle/180)

• Risk classified using Natural Breaks

Results

High-risk areas were identified near sharp curves and rough pavement sections.

Moderate-risk areas were observed in transition curve sections.

Low-risk areas were found in straight road segments with good pavement condition.
